About Me

My name is Andrew Adiletta and I grew up in Bolton, Massachusetts. I have an older brother Matty, a younger brother Jack, and a younger sister Katie. I am currently pursuing a BS/MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), with a specialization in cybersecurity. My education and experience at WPI have given me a strong foundation in various programming languages, such as Java, JavaScript, C#, C/C++, PHP, SQL, Python, and more. I have also had the opportunity to work at Intel, where I designed and implemented fault-tolerant validation utilities and binomial options pricing systems.

In my free time, I love running and playing music. Being physically active helps me maintain a balanced lifestyle and provides a great way to unwind. I also enjoy playing different musical instruments, which allows me to express my creativity and passion for music. I am a big Star Trek nerd and a fan of science fiction, which fuels my imagination and inspires me to think beyond the ordinary.

As a dedicated student and a creative thinker, I am always eager to learn and explore new technologies. I am excited to leverage my skills and experience to contribute to the field of Electrical and Computer Engineering, and I look forward to working on projects that make a positive impact on society.
